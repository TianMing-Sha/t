Authors: Tianming Sha, Haitao Wang, Hejun Wu

Jan. 2024 - Aug. 2024 | AAAI 2025 (Under review)

TimeKAN: A Transparent KAN-Based Approach for Multivariate Time Series Forecasting

Authors: Zechuan Chen, Tianming Sha, Ziyi Tang, Keze Wang

Aug. 2024 - Oct. 2024 | ICLR 2025 (Under review)

We present a new approach TimeKAN which is an interpretable, KAN-based model for multivariate time series forecasting that uses fewer parameters and a novel multi-scale patching module to achieve state-of-the-art predictive performance
